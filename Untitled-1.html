Pastor Dr. Mezgebu A. Tucho

Pastor Dr. Mezgebu A. Tucho holds a doctorate in theology and was born in **Danbi Dolo**, a city in **West Wollega, Oromia, Ethiopia**. He is a devout believer in the **Protestant Church** and currently resides in **Edmonton, Canada**. Despite living in Canada, he returns to Ethiopia annually to teach and preach to his followers, maintaining a strong connection with his homeland. Pastor Mezgebu is a dedicated family man, with **two sons** and **one daughter**. He is also the founder of **Nehemiah Online School**, an online theological education platform. The school has grown significantly, with over **2,000 students** having graduated from the program. This platform allows him to continue teaching and spreading theological knowledge globally, particularly within the Ethiopian community..

Pastor Dr. Mezgebu A. Tucho is a notable Ethiopian theologian and pastor who teaches theology, focusing on conflict transformation and peacebuilding. He has a strong academic and practical background in these areas, as reflected in his book, "Principles of Conflict Transformation". In this work, he outlines a theological approach to resolving conflicts, especially within congregational settings, aiming for deep personal transformation among the involved parties, which goes beyond mere conflict resolution. Dr. Mezgebu has also been involved in significant efforts to equip Ethiopian leaders, youth, and families with skills in anger management and discipleship. He has conducted workshops and seminars in Ethiopia, particularly during challenging times such as the COVID-19 pandemic and local conflicts, teaching principles of peace and emotional control through Christian teachings. He has been part of a larger Mennonite network, working toward spiritual formation and leadership training both in Ethiopia and abroad​( Canadian Mennonite )​( Xlibris )..
